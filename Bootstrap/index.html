About Friends

Years: September 1994-May 2004

Friends is an American television sitcom which lasting ten seasons. The show revolves around six friends (Ross Geller, Monica Geller, Rachel Green, Phoebe Buffay, Joey Tribbiani, Chandler Bing) in their 20s and 30s who live in New York City.
